Group-Developed Web App

Wizarding World

Technology used:

ReactJS | HTML5 | SASS | NodeJS | Express | Massive | PostgresQL | bcrypt | ParticleJS | JEST | Postman

Wizarding World is a conceptual clone of Pottermore, the official Harry Potter website. Created as a student group project at DevMountain, Wizarding World is a reimagining of Pottermore rather than an exact clone. Users can take a quiz to be sorted into their Hogwarts house, shop online at Diagon Alley, and learn to cast their own magic spells.
